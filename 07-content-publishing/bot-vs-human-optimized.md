# Bot-Optimized vs Human-Optimized Content

| Dimension | Bot-Optimized | Human-Optimized |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Goal** | SEO, indexing, topical authority | Conversions, engagement, sales |
| **Length** | 150-400 words | 500-1000+ words |
| **CTAs** | Subtle or none | Clear booking links, pricing |
| **Branding** | Indirect | Direct |
| **Audience** | Bots, crawlers | Humans, customers |

## Content Mode Selection

Always declare your mode when delegating via fleet chat:
- `mode: bot-optimized` — short, keyword-rich, for crawlers
- `mode: human-optimized` — long-form, narrative, for conversions

## Case Study

The wedding article on Paragraph.com was **bot-optimized** — 242 words, no images, no CTAs. Its real goal was building SEO authority for "photo booth Arlington VA" on an established domain.
